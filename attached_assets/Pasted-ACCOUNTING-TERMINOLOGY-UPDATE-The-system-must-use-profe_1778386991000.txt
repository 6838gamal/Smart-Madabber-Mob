The decision engine should generate business-friendly insights such as:

* “Inventory level is below minimum threshold”
* “High expense detected in category X”

* “Possible waste detected”

UI LABELING RULES

* Use clear accounting terminology

* Maintain bilingual terminology consistency in Arabic and English

SYSTEM GOAL UPDATE

The app should feel like:

* A smart operational assistant
